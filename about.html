Our Story

What started as a passion for beauty has grown into a trusted name in Rishikesh. Glamourite Academy was built with one vision — to help people feel confident, skilled, and beautiful, inside and out.

The Academy From basics to advanced

we train aspiring artists to turn their dreams into careers. Professional courses in makeup, hair, nails & skin Hands-on training with live models Certification that helps you start anywhere Whether you’re a beginner or upgrading your skills, your journey starts here.

The Salon
Walk into style. Walk out confident.

Our in-house salon offers:

Trendy haircuts & colour Bridal & party makeup
Skin, nail & haircare treatments
All services by certified professionals & students under expert guidance.

Freelancing

At Glamourite Academy, we prepare you to step confidently into the world of freelancing. From taking client bookings to building your personal brand, we guide you through every step. You’ll learn how to price your services, manage travel kits, and carry yourself professionally — whether it’s for bridal makeup, party looks, or on-location shoots. Our goal is to help you become your own boss with skill and confidence.

Customer Promise

At Glamourite Academy, your trust means everything to us. Whether you’re here for a service or a course, we promise a clean, safe, and welcoming environment. Every treatment is done using genuine products with utmost care and hygiene. Our team ensures personal attention, professional service, and a comfortable experience — because you deserve nothing less than the best.
