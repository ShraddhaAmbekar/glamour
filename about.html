About Us

Our Story

Glamourlite is one of the fastest-growing and most renowned salon chains, setting a new standard for hairstyling and beauty services. We pride ourselves on offering luxury, style, and a personalized experience to every client.

History of Glamourlite

Founded in 1989, Glamourlite started as a single salon and has grown into a Pan-India chain. We now operate over 200 locations across the country and employ more than 6000 professionals.

Corporate Vision

Our vision is to provide an unparalleled salon experience by combining top-tier beauty services with a relaxing, luxurious environment. We strive to create moments of beauty that leave our clients feeling rejuvenated and fabulous.

Customer Promise

We are committed to offering personalized services with precision and care. Every visit promises expert hairstyling, skincare, and beauty services in a calm and luxurious setting.
